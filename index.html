Hand crafted deep learning for your most challenging artificial intelligence problems

We combine the latest in machine learning technology with the care and craftsmanship of mathematicians and statisticians of yore.

Sounds interesting...

Hand made, in the heart of San Francisco.

We were drawn together by a calling: We felt that today's artificial intelligence was moving too fast, was mediated by too many layers of abstraction, and was too disconnected from its roots.

So we started Artisanal AI, where we are remedying that with hand crafted AI solutions. At Artisanal AI we do it all from scratch: backprop, adversarial neural nets, stochastic gradient descent are all calculated by hand, step by step, one iteration at a time. We use the tools of our fathers and grandfathers -- calculators, slide rules and even abaci -- to lovingly create machine learning algorithms that may not be as fast, but they are authentic.
